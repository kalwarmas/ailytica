Privacy Policy

We Value your trust. Learn how we handle your data.

Information We Collect
  • Identity: Name, email, and business credentials.
  • Technical: IP address, cookies, and browser metadata.
  • Operations: AI/ERP usage data and analytics logs.
How We Use Information

Your data allows us to provide a seamless AI experience:

  • Personalizing your dashboard analytics.
  • Processing service requests and demo bookings.
  • Communicating critical updates and newsletters.
Your Data Rights

Under global data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Ask for immediate deletion (Right to be forgotten).
  • Opt-out of automated decision-making and marketing.
Security Protocols

We employ AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS for data in transit. Our servers are audited monthly for security vulnerabilities.
