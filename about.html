Who We Are

We are not another IT vendor trying to sell you more software licenses.

We are the consultancy that starts by telling GCC enterprises the uncomfortable truth: most of their digital transformation budgets are wasted on over-customized ERP disasters, pilot-stage AI that never reaches production, and glossy roadmaps that gather dust.

Ailytica exists to stop that cycle.

We help construction companies, real-estate developers and hospitality groups in Saudi Arabia, UAE and Qatar get the operational control and decision speed they actually need — using Microsoft Dynamics 365 and targeted AI — without the usual 300K–1M SAR failure price tag.

Our Point of View

Most consultancies in the Gulf promise "digital excellence" and deliver months of workshops followed by unmaintainable custom code.

We believe the opposite:

  • 80% of the value comes from standard processes + clean data + disciplined adoption
  • The remaining 20% comes from selective, production-grade AI that solves one painful problem extremely well
  • Anything beyond that is usually ego, vendor incentives, or fear of saying "no" to the client

We say "no" when necessary — and clients respect us for it.

Our Commitment

For every engagement we make four hard promises:

  • ✓ No custom code unless it is mathematically proven to deliver >3× ROI within 12 months
  • ✓ No AI deployment until governance, data quality and user trust are already in place
  • ✓ No project goes live without a realistic adoption plan that includes Arabic-first training and Saudization compliance
  • ✓ If we cannot deliver 15–30% measurable improvement in efficiency, forecasting accuracy or decision speed — we tell you before you sign
