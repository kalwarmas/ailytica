SMS Communication Disclosure

In accordance with GCC telecommunication regulations, Ailytica provides full transparency regarding our mobile outreach. By providing your mobile number during an ERP strategy session or demo request, you acknowledge:

  • Consent: You explicitly authorize Ailytica to send operational updates, AI insights, and Vision 2030-related roadmap updates via SMS.
  • Frequency: Messages are sent based on your project status or engagement level (e.g., meeting reminders or system alerts).
  • Opt-Out: You can text "STOP" at any time to cancel. You can also email info@ailytica.com for manual removal.
  • Rates: Standard carrier message and data rates may apply.
